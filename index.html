FEATURED ATTRACTIONS

Aquatic Odyssey

Dive into an underwater adventure in this massive, futuristic aquarium complex. Visitors explore a series of interconnected domes, each showcasing a different aquatic ecosystem, from vibrant coral reefs to mysterious deep-sea trenches.

The Great Fall

Prepare for the ultimate adrenaline rush as you embark on 'The Great Fall,' an awe-inspiring ride that takes the excitement to new heights - quite literally! This towering adventure promises an exhilarating ascent followed by an adrenaline-pumping plunge that will leave you breathless and craving more.

TimeWarp Theater

A state-of-the-art immersive theater experience that transports audiences through various eras and historical events. Using cutting-edge technology, this multisensory show combines live actors, holographic displays, and special effects to bring history to life.
